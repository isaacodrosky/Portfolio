About Isaac

Hi, I'm Isaac. I am a photographer and web developer currently working toward a Web Development certificate from Wake Technical Community College. I graduated from Appalachian State University in 2018 with a Bachelor's degree in Psychology with the expectation of becoming a counselor or ASL interpreter, but have found myself pursuing work in more creative fields instead.

Thanks to my two photographer parents, I've had a camera in my hand since I was young. While in college, I pursued a minor in photography and began doing freelance and personal photo projects, my favorite being live events and film photography.

In late 2021, I began teaching myself web development in hopes of finding a new career path. With a background in photography and graphic design, I immediately found a passion for creating websites that are functional without compromising good design.

I currently live in Carrboro, NC with my beautiful wife and our three cats. When I'm not taking photos or building websites, you can find me camping, reading, or playing Stardew Valley.
